Ratel — Privacy Policy

Effective: 11 June 2026 · Contact: rajasekarjavaee@gmail.com

What we collect

  • Account: email address and display name (for sign-in).
  • Learning data: lesson progress, answers, XP, streaks, review schedule, achievements, friend codes — to provide the service.
  • AI Coach messages: conversation text you send to the coach is processed to generate replies and is rate-limit counted; it is not used to train models by us.
  • Push token: if you allow notifications, a device token is stored to send learning reminders.
  • Analytics & crash reports: anonymous usage events (Google Analytics for Firebase) and crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics).

Processors

Data is stored with Supabase (database & authentication) and Google Firebase (analytics, crash reporting, push). AI Coach replies are generated via OpenAI's API.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the app (Profile), or by emailing us. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Children

Ratel is suitable for general audiences; we do not knowingly collect more data from children than from any user, and no advertising is shown.

Changes

We will update this page when practices change.
